## Market Information

Retrieves the current prices and general market information for cryptocurrencies.

- **URL**: `/market`
- **Method**: GET
- **Response**: Returns a JSON object with the current prices and general market information for cryptocurrencies. The information is fetched from the CoinGecko API.
